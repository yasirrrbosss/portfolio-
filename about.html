First, what is Web3 and Blockchain?

Blockchain

A decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across multiple computers. It's transparent, secure, and immutable - meaning once data is recorded, it cannot be changed.

Web3

The next generation of the internet built on blockchain technology. It gives users ownership of their data, eliminates middlemen, and creates new economic opportunities.

NFTs

Non-Fungible Tokens represent unique digital ownership on the blockchain. They enable creators to monetize digital art, build communities, and create new revenue models.
